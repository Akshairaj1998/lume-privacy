Privacy Policy Last updated: June 27, 2026 This Privacy Policy explains how LUME ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the LUME mobile application. By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy. 1. Information We Collect We collect the following information when you use LUME: Account information: Your email address, display name, profile photo, bio, and location (optional). Collection data: Watch details you add including brand, model, purchase price, purchase date, photos, and notes. Dream watch data: Watches you mark as grail goals and savings progress. Community content: Posts, polls, wrist check photos, and comments you publish in the community feed. Reminders: Service and maintenance reminders you create for your watches. Usage data: App interactions collected anonymously via Firebase Analytics to help us improve the app. Purchase information: Subscription status managed through RevenueCat. We do not store your payment card details. 2. How We Use Your Information To provide and operate the LUME app and its features To sync your data across devices via your account To send you transactional emails (welcome, reminders, and product updates) To manage your subscription and grant access to premium features To improve the app using anonymised analytics data To enable community features such as posts, respects, and follows 3. Data Storage and Security Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform with row-level security. All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS. Profile photos and watch images are stored in Supabase Storage. We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. 4. Third-Party Services LUME uses the following third-party services: Supabase — database and authentication (Privacy Policy) Firebase Analytics — anonymous usage analytics (Privacy Policy) RevenueCat — subscription management (Privacy Policy) Resend — transactional email delivery (Privacy Policy) Google Play — app distribution and in-app purchases 5. Data Sharing We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared with the third-party services listed above, which are necessary to operate the app. Community content you post (wrist checks, posts, comments) is visible to other LUME users by default. 6. Your Rights You have the right to: Access the personal data we hold about you Update or correct your profile information at any time within the app Delete your account and all associated data by contacting us Opt out of marketing emails by contacting us 7. Children's Privacy LUME is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it. 8. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. 9.
